Heritage Residential offers affordable housing to a variety of individuals with diverse needs. We cater to all populations in our individual homes including Veterans, Seniors, Sober living, Transitional and individuals who are disabled or have mental health conditions. Our homes can be your home for a short period or long term.

Affordable housing to individuals with diverse needs

Why Choose Heritage Homes?

All Inclusive Shared Living

For one monthly payment you do not have to worry about multiple bills. All our payments include a shared room, electric, cable, Internet/wifi, telephone line, water, washer & dryer so you can only worry about getting yourself back on your feet.

Third party Billing

Monthly billing handled by third party to ensure compliance.

Full Kitchen access

A full Kitchen is available for your use. refrigerator, microwave and stove are available for use. Meal arrangements can be arranged

Health and Personal Care Services

You have access to a wide variety of services provided by outsourced third parties of your choice.

    These include:
  • Doctor / Nurse home visits
  • Home health services (RN, Physical, Occupational and Speech Therapy)
  • Hospice
  • Durable medical equipment
  • Case management & Social work
  • Behavioral counseling and therapy
  • Medication management

Hygiene Package

A hygiene package is given to each resident on arrival and as needed.

    Our package includes:
  • Toothbrush, toothpaste
  • A bar of soap, shampoo, conditioner
  • Body lotion, shave gel, razor, deodorant
  • Comb, brush, lip balm facecloth,
  • Hand sanitizer, tissues
  • Nail clippers, hair ties
  • Feminine hygiene products
